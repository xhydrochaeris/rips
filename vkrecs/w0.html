Week 0 : V.A. - Collection of LOVE INSANE
(Vasalla (覇叉羅) & WITH SEXY)

Label/Cat#: None – VW-150530

Released: May 30, 2015

Format: CD, Compilation

Tracklist:

  1. Vasalla (覇叉羅) - 氷の微笑
  2. Vasalla (覇叉羅) - AUTISTIC SYNDROME
  3. Vasalla (覇叉羅) - HIDE OUT
  4. Vasalla (覇叉羅) - 加虐性精神分裂症
  5. WITH SEXY - Deja vu
  6. WITH SEXY - For Solitude
  7. WITH SEXY - 水面華
  8. WITH SEXY - MISTY

A compilation of tracks by Vasalla and WITH SEXY from rare early demo tapes.

Vasalla's part is all four tracks from their 1994 DT 〔Vajra〕

WITH SEXY's part is a reissue of their first two DTs, Deja vu and For Solitude, the first track of their third DT 束縛と嫉妬 憂鬱と悲しみ, and the second track of their DT PRECIOUS LOVE / MISTY
